# Ipolongo Platform
Ipolongo platform is a suite of integrated, cloud-based applications designed to manage the end-to-end delivery of large-scale public health campaigns, such as the distribution of insecticide-treated nets (ITNs) and Seasonal Malaria Chemoprevention (SMC) medicines to underserved populations. 
Ipolongo Platform, is designed to managing the Malaria ITN campaign implimentation in Nigeria, powered by GHSC-PSM SID team, sponsored by PMI.
